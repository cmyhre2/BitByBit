Product vision

Build a weather companion people trust — precise, local, and simple — that helps users make smarter day-to-day decisions and stay safe during severe conditions. We combine local sensors, ensemble models, and human-centered design to deliver timely guidance and actionable insights.

Tagline
WeatherWise — Smarter local forecasts.
Brief description

WeatherWise is a cross-platform weather app delivering real-time conditions, trusted multi-day forecasts, and customizable alerts — designed to help individuals and businesses plan around the weather.

Who it's designed for
  • Everyday users: commuters, students, parents needing reliable daily forecasts.
  • Outdoor enthusiasts: hikers, runners, and travelers wanting detailed hourly info.
  • Professionals: event planners, farmers, construction managers who need alerts and planning tools.
  • Tech-savvy users: power users who benefit from location-based personalization and push notifications.

Most compelling features & benefits

Features below are chosen to directly benefit our target users — each feature includes the user benefit so the value is clear.

Real-time weather updates

Live temp, humidity & wind

Benefit: Make immediate decisions (commute choice, outdoor start time) using station-synced live data and rapid refresh.

7–10 day forecasts

Hourly & daily planning

Benefit: Prepare for multi-day plans and weekend events with detailed hourly breakdowns and trend insights.

Severe weather alerts

Storms, heat waves, tornadoes

Benefit: Receive early, actionable alerts to protect people and assets — crucial for event planners and farmers.

Personalization & favorites

Saved cities & preferences

Benefit: Quickly check multiple locations (home, work, travel destinations) and get tailored notifications.

Activity suggestions

Plan around the weather

Benefit: Know the best windows for hiking, running, or scheduling outdoor events based on forecast confidence.

Push notifications & widgets

Always informed

Benefit: Instant changes to weather conditions delivered to your lock screen and home screen for fast glanceability.

Privacy & data

Privacy-first approach: opt-in location services, clear data use explanations, and the ability to delete account data at any time.
