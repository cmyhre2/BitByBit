Scenario 1 — Daily Weather Interactions

A set of short scenarios illustrating how WeatherWise enhances Emma’s daily routines — from morning prep to weekend planning — with actionable, personalized weather guidance.

Scenario 1.1: Morning Preparation

Emma wakes up at 6:30am and checks WeatherWise while getting ready. The app shows her home is 52°F but her office downtown will be 58°F by arrival, with a notification: “Light jacket recommended – temperature rising throughout day.”

Scenario 1.2: Afternoon Commute Alert

At 4:45pm, Emma receives a push notification: “Heavy rain starting in 25 minutes at your work location. Bring umbrella for commute home.” She grabs her umbrella from her desk drawer before heading out.

Scenario 1.3: Weekend Plans

Saturday morning, Emma checks the app to see if she can walk to the farmers market. WeatherWise shows: “Perfect walking weather until 2pm, then scattered showers. Best window: 9am–1pm.”

Design Insights

  • Provide time-sensitive, location-specific notifications that anticipate user needs.
  • Offer comparative temperature data between home, work, and planned destinations.
  • Use concise, advisory phrasing for quick decisions (e.g., “Bring umbrella,” “Perfect walking weather”).
  • Highlight best activity windows to support lifestyle-oriented planning.
