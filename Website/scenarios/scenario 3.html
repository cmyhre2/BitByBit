Scenario 3 — Family Weather Planning

A scenario series demonstrating how WeatherWise helps Jennifer manage her family’s day — from school mornings to after-school activities and spontaneous playtime.

Scenario 3.1: School Morning Rush

Jennifer checks WeatherWise at 7am. Widget shows: “Kids need jackets + rain gear by 3pm pickup. Temperature drop from 68° to 54° with afternoon showers.”

Scenario 3.2: Activity Conflict Warning

Jennifer's calendar shows soccer practice at 5pm. At noon, WeatherWise notifies: “Thunderstorms likely during soccer practice (5–6pm). Check with coach about cancellation.”

Scenario 3.3: Spontaneous Park Trip

After school, kids want to go to the park. Jennifer glances at her widget: “Good park weather next 2 hours. Rain starting at 5pm.” They head out immediately.

Design Insights

  • Provide actionable, family-focused notifications that summarize complex forecasts into clear guidance.
  • Integrate calendar-based weather conflict alerts for scheduled activities.
  • Enable quick-glance widgets showing concise visuals like “rain gear needed” or “good park weather.”
  • Emphasize simplicity and context-relevance for multitasking parents.
