Persona 1 — The Morning Commuter

A persona representing busy professionals who rely on accurate, time-sensitive weather information for daily commutes.

Overview

Emma is a 32-year-old marketing professional from Louisville who drives 30 minutes from the suburbs to downtown each day. Her work schedule and preparation depend heavily on timely, location-aware weather information.

Persona Details

Name: Emma

Age: 32

Occupation: Marketing Professional

Location: Louisville, KY

Profile Summary: Emma commutes daily from the suburbs to downtown Louisville, where conditions often differ between start and destination. She checks her iPhone's default weather app twice a day but still gets caught unprepared due to forecasts lacking commute-specific insights.

Goals:

  • Receive alerts only for actionable changes, such as rain near commute times.
  • Get accurate, location-based forecasts for both home and work.
  • Save time during busy mornings by getting concise outfit or gear suggestions.

Frustrations:

  • Generic forecasts that don’t align with commute timing.
  • Inaccurate predictions leading to unpreparedness (e.g., unexpected rain).
  • Needing to interpret temperature data instead of receiving clear advice.

Needs:

  • Commute-aware alerts like “Rain starting in 20 minutes.”
  • Guidance phrased in simple, actionable language (e.g., “light jacket recommended”).
  • Forecast comparison between home and work locations.
Persona Snapshot
Name
Emma — The Morning Commuter
A busy professional balancing work and personal routines, who values fast, reliable, and relevant weather updates.

Motivation: Stay informed without wasting time.

Tech Use: iPhone default weather app, light notifications, prefers simplicity.

Key takeaway
Emma values accuracy, simplicity, and relevance in weather insights—she needs forecasts that fit her commute, not just her city.

Design implications

  • Introduce “commute timeline” view for home-to-work weather differences.
  • Use clear, actionable alerts instead of static temperature notifications.
  • Offer quick outfit suggestions for busy mornings.
